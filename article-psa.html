An official statement from UE Zenith Esports Caloocan regarding the disaffiliation of Mr. Suncell De Leon from the organization.

UE Zenith Esports Caloocan is issuing this public statement in the interest of full transparency and to protect the integrity of the organization, its members, and the community it serves.

// Official Public Service Announcement — UE Zenith Esports Caloocan
// Official Statement

UE ZENITH ESPORTS CALOOCAN would like to formally inform the public that Mr. Suncell De Leon, former President of the organization, is no longer affiliated with UE Zenith Esports Caloocan in any capacity.

Effective immediately, Mr. De Leon has no authority, involvement, or connection with the organization's projects, teams, management, partnerships, operations, or activities, whether directly or indirectly.

Furthermore, he is not authorized to represent, use, or associate himself with UE Zenith Esports Caloocan's name, branding, logos, intellectual property, or any other organizational assets in any form.

This statement is being made to ensure that all members of the community, partner organizations, and the public at large are fully informed of Mr. De Leon's current standing with UE Zenith Esports Caloocan. Any actions taken under the organization's name without proper authorization will be treated accordingly.

UE Zenith Esports Caloocan remains committed to the principles of integrity, transparency, and accountability that have guided this organization. The leadership, members, and community of UE Zenith will continue to move forward with those values at the core of everything we do.

// Report Misuse

Should there be any information, evidence, or reports of the continued use or misrepresentation of the organization's name, identity, or intellectual property — the public is strongly encouraged to report this to UE Zenith Esports Caloocan through our official Facebook page at facebook.com/uezencal or via the organization's official email address at [email protected].

We appreciate the continued support and cooperation of our community in helping us protect the integrity and interests of UE Zenith Esports Caloocan. For guidance and transparency.
