OUR HISTORY

UE Zenith is a student-led esports organization dedicated to fostering talent, collaboration, and innovation in the competitive gaming scene.

From humble beginnings, we've grown into a thriving community where players, strategists, and creators come together to push boundaries and achieve excellence.

At Zenith, we believe in nurturing not only gaming skills but also leadership, teamwork, and personal growth. Every tournament is an opportunity to rise, learn, and shine.

MISSION & VISION

MISSION

To empower student gamers and content creators by providing opportunities to develop their skills, compete at high levels, and contribute to a supportive, innovative esports community. We strive to inspire passion, promote sportsmanship, and create pathways for players to grow academically, professionally, and competitively.

VISION

We envision a dynamic, inclusive esports ecosystem where every member can reach their personal zenith. By combining talent, teamwork, and creativity, we aim to be recognized not only for competitive achievements but also for shaping a vibrant, supportive, and forward-thinking community nationwide.

// Our Standards

CODE OF CONDUCT

01
INCLUSIVITY & RESPECT

All members must foster a welcoming and respectful environment. Discrimination, harassment, or toxic behavior is strictly prohibited.

02
COMMITMENT

Members are expected to attend meetings, practices, tournaments, and events punctually. Consistent participation ensures team growth and success.

03
ACADEMIC BALANCE

While competitive gaming is important, academic responsibilities come first. Members must maintain satisfactory academic performance.

04
CONTENT & BRANDING

Members represent UE Zenith in all public content. Any media, posts, or communications must reflect the organization's values and branding guidelines.

05
FAIR PLAY

Integrity and sportsmanship are essential. Cheating, exploiting glitches, or unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated under any circumstances.
