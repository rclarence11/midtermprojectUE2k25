UE Zenith is part of the FINAL THREE on Predator Gaming's Campus Rivals 2025. The crown is within reach.

Out of every collegiate esports team in the country, only three remain standing. UE Zenith Esports – Caloocan is one of them. The team has punched their way through the bracket of Predator Gaming's Campus Rivals 2025, and they are now among the Final Three competing for the title of Campus Legends.

This is not luck. This is the product of relentless preparation, unwavering team chemistry, and a competitive mindset that was forged through months of early mornings, late nights, and sessions that didn't end until the team felt ready. Every win in this tournament was earned — none of it was given.

"Three teams left. One crown. UE Zenith is ready."

// UE Zenith Caloocan — Predator Gaming Campus Rivals 2025, Final Three

Campus Rivals 2025, presented by Predator Gaming, is one of the most prestigious collegiate esports tournaments in the Philippines. The competition draws top-tier university teams from across the country, making a Final Three finish a massive achievement on its own. But UE Zenith isn't here just to compete — they're here to finish what they started.

// Tournament Milestone

UE Zenith Caloocan — Predator Gaming Campus Rivals 2025 — Final Three

The atmosphere around the team has been electric. Players and staff are dialed in. The preparation has been sharp and detailed. Every opponent that stands between UE Zenith and the championship has been studied, analyzed, and planned against.

The community has rallied behind the team every step of the way — and this is the moment that support matters most. UE Zenith is representing not just their campus, but every player who ever believed that Philippine collegiate esports belongs on the biggest stages.

The campus legends are not just on the way. They're already here.
